ASI:ONE Web3 Native LLM

ASI-1 Mini is the world’s first Web3-native large language model (LLM), engineered for autonomous, agentic workflows. It’s designed to think, adapt, and collaborate, ensuring your AI-driven workflows are smarter than ever. Developed by the Artificial Superintelligence Alliance, ASI-1 Mini is compact, fast, and built for real-world natural language applications. With integrated Knowledge Graphs, it delivers context-aware, adaptive interactions—ideal for powering AI agents, processing documents, and scaling decentralized systems.
