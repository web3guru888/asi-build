FAQ

Home » About » FAQ

Explore our FAQs to learn more about the ASI Alliance—our decentralized approach, governance, and innovative AI ecosystem. Find clear, concise answers to common questions and discover how we’re shaping the future of AI together.

What is the core mission of the ASI Alliance?

The ASI Alliance aims to become the largest open-source, independent entity in Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) research and infrastructure. Its primary objective is to develop AGI in an accessible, ethical, and decentralized manner, ensuring that the benefits of this technology are widely distributed.

How does the ASI Alliance leverage blockchain technology to enhance the security, scalability, and accessibility of AI systems?

Blockchain technology serves as a financial settlement layer, enabling secure and transparent transactions across the network. Additionally, it provides essential developer utilities such as the Almanac smart contract, which ensures agent discoverability and coordination within the ecosystem.

What are the key advantages of decentralizing artificial intelligence?

  • Ensuring broad accessibility and preventing monopolization of AI capabilities.
  • Establishing a credible alternative to major technology companies that currently dominate AI research and development.
  • Distributing profits generated from AI models to token holders rather than centralizing them among a select few entities.

How does the ASI Alliance ensure AI development aligns with the principles of open-source collaboration and decentralization?

The ASI Alliance operates transparently, with nearly all developer tools being open-source and publicly accessible. This ensures that research and technology are not restricted, fostering a collaborative environment where innovation thrives.

What governance mechanisms does the ASI Alliance implement to enable transparent, community-driven decision-making?

The alliance employs blockchain-based voting systems, allowing token holders and stakers to participate in governance. This decentralized approach empowers the community to influence key decisions through open proposals and democratic consensus.

How does the ASI Alliance integrate tokenomics and incentives to encourage participation from Web3 community members?

The alliance has integrated the FET token as:

  • An access deposit token for premium product features.
  • A medium of exchange within the AI agent marketplace.
  • A governance mechanism, granting token holders a voice in decision-making processes.
  • A central mechanism to ensure the security of the Fetch.ai mainnet, which operates on a Cosmos SDK-based Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism.

How does the ASI Alliance approach AI ethics, bias mitigation, and responsible deployment in a decentralized environment?

The ASI Alliance is committed to ethical AI development by ensuring transparency in training data, promoting diverse and inclusive model development, and leveraging decentralized governance to establish fair and unbiased policies.

What are the biggest technical challenges in merging AI with blockchain, and how does the ASI Alliance plan to address them?

Challenges include computational efficiency, data storage, and technical complexity. The alliance is tackling these by developing a vertically integrated technology suite that offers a seamless development experience, alongside ongoing research, experimentation, and iterative progress.

How does the ASI Alliance ensure interoperability between different blockchain networks to enable seamless AI adoption?

By leveraging the Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) protocol and strategic bridges to networks such as Ethereum, Cardano, and Binance Smart Chain, the ASI Alliance facilitates seamless cross-chain interactions for AI-based applications.

What strategies are in place to attract developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs to contribute to the ASI ecosystem?

The ASI Alliance fosters engagement through:

  • Hosting regular hackathons and developer challenges.
  • Providing comprehensive, well-documented resources.
  • Offering near 24/7 online support via community channels like Discord and Telegram.
  • Reducing technical complexity by creating user-friendly interfaces, simplifying integration, and optimizing the overall developer experience.

How does the alliance plan to address energy efficiency concerns associated with running AI models on decentralized infrastructure?

Through partnerships with compute providers like CUDOS, the ASI Alliance has established a distributed compute marketplace, enabling individuals and enterprises to contribute excess computing power, thereby optimizing energy consumption.

What partnerships and collaborations does the ASI Alliance see as critical for advancing decentralized AI on a global scale?

The alliance actively seeks partnerships with researchers, blockchain networks, enterprises, and governments to expand the decentralized AI ecosystem and drive widespread adoption.

How will the ASI Alliance measure success, and what key milestones should Web3 community members track?

Key success metrics include:

  • Delivering AI models that match or surpass industry benchmarks.
  • Achieving strong user adoption and positive community feedback.
  • Ensuring equitable profit distribution among stakeholders as a result of successful product deployment.

What opportunities does the ASI Alliance create for Web3 contributors, from developers to investors, in shaping the decentralized AI revolution?

The alliance provides multiple avenues for participation, including education, mentorship, and integration opportunities for developers, researchers, and investors looking to contribute to the decentralized AI movement.

How does the ASI Alliance differentiate itself from other decentralized AI initiatives?

The ASI Alliance stands out through its industry-first vertically integrated technology suite, years of research, and leadership in AI infrastructure, applications, developer resources, and research and development.

How can developers integrate their AI solutions into the ASI Alliance’s network?

Developers can integrate by reviewing the documentation, understanding the technological offerings, and strategically migrating components such as compute for larger solutions. Smaller projects can be onboard by reformatting code, recreating applications, or integrating with existing APIs.

What mechanisms are in place to ensure data privacy in AI models deployed on blockchain networks?

The alliance employs multiple privacy layers, including cryptographic primitives leveraged in blockchain technology, and is actively developing additional solutions to enhance privacy and security.

How does the ASI Alliance’s approach to AGI differ from centralized research institutions like OpenAI or DeepMind?

The ASI Alliance is pioneering new AI model architectures combined with distributed infrastructure to ensure decentralization, distinguishing it from centralized institutions.

What industries stand to benefit the most from ASI Alliance’s decentralized AI solutions?

AI is a general-purpose technology, akin to electricity, meaning virtually every industry and developmental sector can benefit from the ASI Alliance’s decentralized AI solutions.

How does the ASI Alliance ensure compliance with evolving global AI regulations?

By collaborating with leading law firms, staying informed on regulatory changes, and continuously adapting its project framework to maintain compliance.

What role does machine learning model explainability and interpretability play in the ASI ecosystem?

A crucial role—the alliance is actively working to mitigate the “black box” problem, which is particularly challenging with larger AI models.

How does the alliance plan to educate and onboard non-technical users into its ecosystem?

By simplifying onboarding through user-friendly interfaces, reducing technical complexity, and offering more cost-effective AI models, products, and infrastructure.

What funding mechanisms, apart from tokenomics, support the ASI Alliance’s growth and development?

Revenue from existing products and tokenomics are the primary funding sources, ensuring continuous development and expansion.

How does the ASI Alliance ensure the long-term sustainability of its decentralized AI infrastructure?

Through frequent audits, adherence to best security practices, rigorous testing, and implementing robust safeguards against malicious activities.

How will the ASI Alliance measure and communicate its progress to stakeholders and the wider world?

The alliance actively engages the community through platforms like Telegram, Discord, X (formerly Twitter), Reddit, GitHub, and others, ensuring accessibility to updates, resources, and transparent communication.

Which blockchain networks is the ASI Alliance interoperable with?

The ASI Alliance supports interoperability with multiple networks, including Ethereum, Cardano, Binance Smart Chain, and Cosmos via the IBC protocol.

What are the primary wallets recommended for users within the ASI ecosystem?

Users are encouraged to utilize wallets that support Cosmos-based assets, such as Keplr, as well as wallets that integrate with Ethereum and other supported chains.

What is the main token of the ASI Alliance?

The primary token for the ASI Alliance is FET. Despite the merger the token ticker hasn’t been changed! This will come in time.

When was the ASI Alliance formed, and who were the founding members?

The ASI Alliance was formed through the merger of Fetch.ai, Ocean Protocol, and SingularityNET in April of 2024.

Have there been additional mergers following the initial formation of the ASI Alliance?

Yes, following the first merger between the founding members Fetch.ai, Ocean protocol and SingularityNET, the Alliance has further merged with CUDOS in October of 2024.

About us

The Artificial Superintelligence Alliance (ASI) is a pioneering collaboration dedicated to decentralized artificial intelligence. Formed from the token merger of Fetch.ai, SingularityNET, Ocean Protocol, and later joined by CUDOS, the Alliance is uniquely positioned to democratize AI by combining cutting-edge blockchain infrastructure with open-source AI development. ASI aims to deliver ethical, accessible, and transparent AI technologies, empowering communities and ensuring the benefits of AI are equitably shared.
